Welcome to Word Search!!

Our web app is inspired by Leetcode problem no. 79. Word Search.
Using the concept of Backtracking, our app allows users to search for words within a maze of letters.
Users can input a word, and our app will navigate through the maze, seeking a path that matches the word.
It's a fun and challenging way to exercise problem-solving skills while enjoying a word search game experience.
